Welcome to Tech Show @ LimsLab!

Native IM-MS+

As a key structural mass spectrometric tool, Native IM-MS is greatly comprementary to probing proein structure with unprecedented analytical speed and great economics in sample consumption.

In-cell MS+

In-cell MS is a new in situ MS tool, proposed in 2018 by Li et al. and desinged for rapid protein and protein complex identification from single living cells.

Tagerted Proteomics+

Targeted Proteomics is a most traditional and best established, large-scale MS strategy to analyze proteome with most attainable throughput so far.
